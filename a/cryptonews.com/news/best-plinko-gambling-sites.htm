What is Plinko?

Plinko is a fun game that sees players release balls through a board containing a number of pins, typically between 12 and 16. The pins will change the direction the ball is falling, eventually guiding it into a hole. Generally, the prizes in a game of Plinko will be tied to the resting place of the ball. Some Plinko boards will offer straight cash prizes while others multiply the bet amount.
