When funding a Bitcoin betting account, players can easily securely deposit funds – the process is much the same as funding a crypto exchange account.

This is in stark contrast to how traditional sportsbooks work, which require players to manually enter their debit/credit card details to make a deposit.

When it comes to payment speeds, the best Bitcoin betting sites offer lightning-fast deposits and withdrawals. 

Regarding the former, leading providers in this space will credit the betting account after just one blockchain confirmation – which typically takes 10 minutes.  Withdrawals are even more impressive, as the best Bitcoin betting sites will instantly approve a cash-out request.
