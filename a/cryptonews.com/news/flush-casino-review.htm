Flush Casino Welcome Bonus Offers

Like all online casinos, Flush provides a sign-up promo for all newly registered customers. The Flush Casino welcome offer comes in two tiers, depending on how much crypto you deposit:

To claim the incentive, you only need to make the required deposit. Once you head to the Wallet, the site will prompt you about the bonus, and you can select it if you wish. There’s no Flush Casino bonus code to worry about, only the deposit and confirmation.

Like all deposit promos, these incentives come with wagering requirements and a few other terms:

  • Tier 1 bonus — 30x wagering requirements, $0.1 min bet and $5 max bet
  • Tier 2 bonus — 35x wagering requirements, $0.2 min bet and $5 max bet

Note the increase in the wagering requirements for the bigger bonus. Bear in mind that the average for the industry is around 30x, so 35x is still reasonable. That said, the wagering requirements apply to both the deposited and the bonus amount, so larger deposits might not be worthwhile.

In this case, it will be easier to complete the requirement by depositing a lower amount or even going for the Tier 1 bonus.

All things considered, Flush is still one of the sites with the best Bitcoin casino bonuses, primarily due to the attractive welcome offer and the VIP program.
