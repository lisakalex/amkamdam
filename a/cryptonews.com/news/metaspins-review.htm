Metaspins Bonuses and Promos


Metaspins offers a couple different promo options. These are available both for new and existing users. Here, we’ll take a closer look at everything that is currently available.

Metaspins Welcome Bonus Offers

The Metaspins welcome offer will match your initial deposit 100%. The maximum amount of the bonus depends on the payment method you choose. The difference can be quite noticeable.

It can go up to 1 BTC if you’re choosing to deposit bitcoins. But for any other payment method, the total will actually vary. The other bonuses go up to 10 ETH, 10,000 USDT, 10,000 USDC, 100 BCH, 200,000 TRX, 35,000 ADA, 150 LTC, and 125,000 DOGE.

For the sake of a short comparison, this makes the maximum bonus available in BTC around triple the amount that you get when you deposit stablecoins.

At the time of writing, the bitcoin bonus was by far the most advantageous, when comparing fiat values. After that came bitcoin cash, TRON, then ether. The rest of the coins were pretty similar, with dogecoin bringing up the rear, as this bonus is worth the least.

The Metaspins welcome bonus actually makes it one of the best crypto casinos with bonuses. No Metaspins bonus codes are required to claim this welcome bonus. However, you will be prompted to opt in when you create an account, so don’t forget to do that.

Metaspins Shitcodes

Shitcodes are codes that casinos share on their social media in order to reward their users, popularized by the BC.Game casino. However, at the time of writing, Metaspins did not offer any known shitcodes.

On the other hand, the point of shitcodes is that they’re not always widely advertised, to reward more dedicated users. We recommend following Metaspins social media to keep up with their posts.

Existing Customer Promos

Metaspins does not have too many existing customer promos at this time. While the Metaspins welcome bonus is generous enough, existing customers will have to make do with only a couple promotions.

One of them is Game of the Week, where playing a certain game nets you double the points needed to level up through their loyalty program. They also list the games that will be eligible for double the points each week in the next month or so.

Finally, there’s the daily MetaLotto. You can buy as many tickets as you want, with a discount for bulk purchases. The jackpot is worth $20,000. You also get a free ticket with every deposit you make at Metaspins.

Metaspins Loyalty Program

The casino does have a loyalty program that anyone can participate in. It requires you to place bets with real money and level up.

Each time you earn points and level up, you get new rewards depending on your new level. Also, if you’re part of the Metaspins VIP program, you will also get a daily Metaspin as long as you play at least $30 at the casino within 24 hours.

Higher levels also get rakeback (up to 50% at the highest level) and reloads (at the highest level, 50% up to $1,000). The reload bonus adds the appropriate amount to your next deposit as a new bonus. It’s always 50%, but the maximum amount you can receive increases as you level up.

Metaspins Interface & User Experience


Our Metaspins review is also taking the entirety of user experience into account. Here, we break down everything we’ve found. The Metaspins casino is quite simple to navigate. On the left hand side of the screen, you’ll find the basic navigation tabs. There are five of them if you’re not logged in; seven if you’re an active user. From there, you can access the casino, MetaLotto, your own rewards, all promotions, the loyalty program, your own account, and support. As you can see, everything you need is neatly organized and accessible from one place.

When you enter the casino, the games at the top will be the games you’ve played most recently (if there are any). Under that are the categories that we’ve mentioned when we reviewed the available selection of games.

Signing up is also simple enough. We’ve mentioned that Metaspins is a no-KYC casino, which means you only need to provide a valid email address. However, you’ll be prompted to create a secure password, with both uppercase and lowercase letters, symbols and numbers. A healthy approach to security is always good to see.

Another feature that Metaspins reviews in general agree is great to see is the number of available languages. Aside from English, you can also use the casino in German, French, Norwegian, Portuguese, Spanish, and Finnish.

One potential drawback we’ve noticed is that the website does not have a scroll bar on its right side, which means you can only scroll using your mouse wheel. This makes scrolling on laptop mousepads somewhat tricky. However, this is admittedly a very minor criticism.

Help is also readily available. There is a Support section on the left hand side of the screen that opens a live chat with their support staff. You can also open it by clicking the Open Chat prompt written at a 90-degree angle on the right hand side of the screen.

It will prompt you to either search their FAQ (another great way to troubleshoot the most common issues) or to enter your information and start chatting. The response times are pretty good, so you won’t be waiting long.
