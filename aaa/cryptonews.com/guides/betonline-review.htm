What is BetOnline?


BetOnline is an online casino and sportsbook that launched in 2001. It’s headquartered in Antigua and Barbuda and licensed in Panama.

BetOnline has offered online betting services to US players for more than 20 years, although as an offshore betting site it’s not licensed to operate in any US state. The platform was also one of the first online gambling sites to accept Bitcoin.

Players will find around 700 casino games and more than 35 sports betting markets at BetOnline. The site also has a racebook for wagering on horse races and hosts live online poker tournaments.

BetOnline Bonuses & Promos


BetOnline offers a range of attractive bonuses to encourage players to join the casino and return for more play. Let’s take a look at all of the bonus offers currently available.

BetOnline offers welcome bonuses for its casino, sportsbook, and poker tournaments.

At the sportsbook, new players can earn a one-time 50% deposit match bonus up to $1,000. This BetOnline welcome bonus requires a $55 minimum deposit and includes a 10x wagering requirement.

For poker play, players receive a 100% deposit match bonus up to $1,000 along with 8 days’ worth of free tournament entry tickets. The tickets can be used to join entry-level tournaments that, if players win, can qualify them for a seat at BetOnline’s weekly $100,000 tournament.

BetOnline Loyalty Program

Casino loyalty programs can be very lucrative for players, and BetOnline’s program is no exception.

The BetOnline VIP program rewards loyal players with rebates, extra bonuses, priority payments, exclusive gifts, and more. It’s one of the most transparent VIP programs we’ve seen as well as one of the most generous.

What Games Can You Play at BetOnline Casino?


BetOnline has a wide range of casino games—around 700 in total. While this is far less than some other top crypto casinos, our BetOnline casino review found that the selection comes from widely respected software providers. We didn’t find ourselves lacking for gaming options.

Slots

BetOnline offers more than 620 slot games from providers like BetSoft, Rival, BetGames, Nucleus Gaming, and Fresh Deck Studios.

The slots library isn’t particularly organized—there’s no way to filter by software provider or slots type. In addition, players can’t try out slot games in demo mode before betting on them.

Roulette

BetOnline has 15 virtual roulette tables and 15 live dealer tables. Variations include European roulette, double ball roulette, and dragon roulette.

Blackjack

Players will find 22 virtual blackjack tables and 27 live tables at BetOnline. There are many variations available including multi-hand blackjack, European blackjack, super 7 blackjack, and 21 burn blackjack.

Poker

BetOnline is a leader in online poker play. At the main BetOnline casino, players will find 9 video poker tables for oasis poker, Caribbean poker, 3-card poker, let it ride, and more.

BetOnline hosts daily live poker tournaments that players must buy into to join. There are weekday tournaments with low stakes, nightly progressive tournaments with higher payouts, and Sunday Majors tournaments with prize pools worth up to $100,000. On weekends, new tournaments start every 15-30 minutes, so players have plenty of chances to play.

Live Dealer Games

BetOnline offers 71 live dealer tables from FreshDeck Studios and Visionary iGaming. Live dealer tables are available 24/7 and include blackjack, roulette, baccarat, Wheel of Fortune, and a lottery game.

There aren’t many live game show games at BetOnline. For example, players won’t find Monopoly, Deal or No Deal, Dreamcatcher, or Candyland.

Other Games

Other BetOnline casino games include craps, keno, dice, and scratch cards. The casino also has specialty video games such as Space Invaders. There are no crash games, like Aviator, available at BetOnline.

BetOnline Sports Betting


BetOnline is one of the largest online sportsbooks available to US players. It offers an enormous range of markets and competitive odds, positioning it to take a major share of the $7.6 billion US sports betting market.

Let’s take a closer look at BetOnline’s sports betting options.

Available Sports & Markets

BetOnline offers betting on all the major US sports leagues along with popular sports like soccer, tennis, golf, rugby, auto racing, UFC, and more. It also carries odds for less common sports markets including:

  • Volleyball
  • Surfing
  • Wrestling
  • Biathlon

Bettors can also wager on US politics, entertainment events, and the outcome of the New York Lottery. In total, BetOnline offers more than 35 betting markets.

For esports betting, players at BetOnline can wager on:

  • NBA2K
  • Mobile Legends
  • Counter-Strike
  • League of Legends
  • Valorant
  • Rainbow Six

Players can even place futures wager on esports at BetOnline, which is relatively unusual among the best Bitcoin sports betting sites.

BetOnline has a large racebook where players can wager on horse races around the world. Bets are available on both thoroughbred and harness races.

Most markets are available for live betting. BetOnline updates odds in real time.

BetOnline Odds

BetOnline offers fairly competitive odds, although they’re rarely the best in the industry. We noticed a slight premium across the board at BetOnline, which makes sense since this is a relatively large and well-established offshore sportsbook. For some less popular sports, BetOnline is the only place players can place bets on upcoming matches.

BetOnline offers a few ways to make sports betting even more interesting. Daily odds boosters offer better-than-average odds for particular wagers across popular sports leagues. The sportsbook displays odds boosts for the upcoming week so bettors can decide what to wager on.

The sportsbook also offers same-game parlays. These are featured parlay bets involving two prop bets from a single match. They typically offer very attractive boosted odds.

BetOnline Interface & User Experience


BetOnline offers an outstanding user experience. The platform is easy to navigate and offers well-organized menus to help players explore all the different betting options. The casino and sportsbook can be accessed from the same site, but are clearly separated so that the betting options are clear.

For poker play, BetOnline offers both a web platform and a downloadable desktop app for Windows and Mac. The tournament schedule is clear and easy to browse, although it would be nice if the site offered more information about the limits, capacity, and approximate duration of each tournament.

One of the few shortcomings we found with BetOnline’s player experience was in the casino. There are menus for different types of BetOnline games, like slots, blackjack, table games, and live dealer games. However, there’s no way to sort games by software provider or popularity. This makes it somewhat challenging to find slot games.

At the sportsbook, BetOnline ensures the bet slip is never more than a click away. Players can watch live streams of most esport matches and a virtual display that shows how other types of sports matches are unfolding.

The site is about to launch a new version of its sportsbook, which looks similar to the existing version but with a modern flair. The most noticeable upgrade is that players can always keep an eye on live matches as they browse other odds available at BetOnline.

Does BetOnline Have a Mobile App?


BetOnline doesn’t offer a mobile app for iOS or Android devices. However, players on the go can still access BetOnline using their smartphone’s browser.

The gambling platform is optimized for mobile, so players can access all of the features they’d find when using BetOnline on a computer. They can make deposits and withdrawals, play casino games, bet on sports, join poker tournaments, and more.

Overall, we found BetOnline’s mobile experience to be quite good even without a dedicated app.

BetOnline Security


BetOnline has been offering online gambling for more than 20 years. It’s licensed in Panama, which has tight rules to ensure player safety. BetOnline has long catered to US players without any major incidents, despite the fact that the site is not licensed in any US state.

BetOnline reviews by current and past players are generally very positive. The gambling site has a 3.6-star rating on Trustpilot after more than 4,000 reviews, which is very good compared to other online betting sites.

BetOnline uses SSL encryption to protect players on its site. Players’ funds are kept in segregated accounts, ensuring that BetOnline never mixes customers’ funds with its own operating funds.

This site follows anti-money laundering rules and requires Know Your Customer (KYC) checks for all new players. This means BetOnline collects personal information and that it’s not possible to bet fully anonymously, even when using cryptocurrency. However, BetOnline has a strong and transparent privacy policy available on its site.
