What is Bizzo Casino?


Bizzo is an online casino for both casual fiat gamers and crypto gamblers. Though relatively new in the space, they’ve grown quite popular.

The casino is notable for its large number of excellent games (including live casino games). The overall design of the website and the UI of the games help to create an enjoyable and smooth experience. In total, more than 70 top-tier software providers are represented, although the casino does not offer any sports betting options at the moment.

TechSolutions Group, the company behind Bizzo Casino, has two different licenses: one from the government of Curacao and one from the Kahnawake Gaming Commission. This also helped establish the casino’s credibility.

Bizzo Casino Bonuses & Promos 


Bizzo Casino has multiple bonus deals offering both free spins and additional funds, but there are certain conditions that have to be met first. Let’s take a closer look.

Bizzo Casino Welcome Bonus Offers

Bizzo Casino welcome bonus is actually a package of benefits a new player can get because it includes both free spins and extra funds. The casino will match any initial deposit of up to €100 and provide 100 free spins for Dig Dig Digger or Mechanical Clover slots.

It’s worth noting that your free spins will be split into two batches – the first half arrives immediately, while the other comes 24 hours after the deposit is made.

To claim this bonus, you only need to create an account and make a deposit. However, the bonus does have a x40 wagering requirement, and the maximum bet is capped at €5 (this only applies if the bonus is active).

Existing Customer Promos

Existing players also have something to look forward to because every deposit made on a Monday will be accompanied by some free spins. 20 free spins are awarded for the minimum deposit (€20), 50 spins for a deposit of €50 or more, and 100 spins for a €100 deposit.

On Thursdays, players can get a reload bonus of 50% (up to €200) usable in Magic Cauldron – The Enchanted Brew.

Bizzo Casino’s welcome offer also extends to a bonus for a player’s second deposit. In this case, they will receive 50% of the funds deposited, up to €300, and 50 free spins, but only for Johnny Cash or Elvis Frog in Vegas. The same wagering conditions as for the welcome bonus apply.

Bizzo Casino Loyalty Program

The Bizzo Casino VIP program consists of 30 levels that players can scale the more they play on the casino’s website. The first five levels award an increasing number of free spins, whereas from level 6 this changes to cash prizes, starting with €5. Level 30 awards a whopping €150,000.

The idea is simple: for every €15 bet on any of the games, players receive one comp point. As the number of comp points grows, so will the player’s VIP level, which means better prizes.

The rewards won this way have to be turned over three times before they can be withdrawn.

Bizzo Casino Bonus Promo Codes


In order to activate Bizzo’s existing customer promos, you must input a specific code when making a deposit. If the conditions are met, the code will activate the bonus. However, there is no Bizzo Casino bonus code needed for the welcome bonus – this is triggered automatically.

Bizzo Casino bonus codes:

  • CASH – to activate the second deposit bonus
  • SPIN – for Monday free spins
  • THU – Thursday reload bonus

Bizzo Casino No Deposit Bonus Codes

Bizzo Casino doesn’t currently offer a no deposit bonus.

What Games Can You Play at Bizzo Casino?


There are more than 3000 Bizzo Casino games available, and pretty much all of them run extremely smoothly. 

Slots

Slots are this casino’s bread and butter as they form the majority of the games (about two-thirds of the titles are slots). In fact, this casino is right up there with the best Bitcoin slots sites. There are plenty of different types of slots to choose from, be that classic slots or jackpot slots, and a range of fun themes including adventure, horror, science fiction, etc.

It’s important to note that only slots contribute to meeting the turnover requirement for the bonuses players receive.

When it comes to some particular titles that caught our eye here, games like Wolf Gold by Pragmatic Play and Felix Gaming’s Book of Annuaki certainly deserve to be mentioned. Then there’s also Johnny Cash, Four Lucky Diamonds, the latter of which offers multiple play areas. After certain conditions are met, you can unlock additional reel sets – which provide more chances to win big.

Roulette

there are plenty of roulette titles at Bizzo to keep players happy. All the main types of roulette are represented, including American, European, French, and fun variations like Bonus roulette. Bizzo Casino partners with game developers that employ random number generators to come up with the outcome for every round. Therefore, players can rest assured every game is 100% fair.

Blackjack

Blackjack is another big group of Bizzo Casino games, and it covers all the most popular sub-genres: Classic, European, American, Atlantic City, and more. 

Some top-quality providers are represented in this category, from Pragmatic Play to Evolution Gaming and Playtech, so the quality of games is perfect. Great graphics and technical perfection create an excellent blackjack experience.

Some of the titles we recommend here are Blackjack Classic 1-72 and Lightning Blackjack by Evolution and Blackjack Lobby by Playtech, but there are many other options, so a little bit of digging will go a long way.

Poker

The only part of Bizzo Casino’s library we have some criticism of is its poker lobby. Sure, there are several different types of poker one can play here, such as Caribbean Stud, Texas Hold ‘Em, and Three Card Poker, but it is clear that the focus was on other types of games.

Live dealer

After slots, live dealer games are the best part of Bizzo Casino’s library. About 15 different developers such as Evolution Gaming and Pragmatic Play deliver more than 200 different titles, so the fans of this type of games can really enjoy themselves. Poker, blackjack, roulette, and baccarat are all covered, while game shows such as Deal or No Deal, Monopoly Live, Dream Catcher, and many others are available, too.

The dealers are very very professional and excel in ensuring you have a fun experience, never crossing the line but always doing their best to keep players engaged and happy. They are certainly one of the main reasons why this part of the website is so popular. The interfaces for each game – like Treasure Island, Crazy Pachinko and Lightning Roulette – we played were well-designed, which made for a fantastic overall experience.

Other games

Apart from all these games, there are a few other genres one can enjoy here. The most prominent example of that is Bizzo Casino’s fast games, which are sometimes referred to as arcade games by other casinos. These include the likes of Plinko, Minesweeper, Aviator, Limbo Cat and others.

There are also various dice and bingo games available.

What Software Does Bizzo Casino Use?


There are over 70 game developers represented at Bizzo Casino, including Pragmatic Play, Evolution Gaming, Microgaming, and Betsoft – all represented by titles as popular as Buffalo King Megaways, Bonanza, Thunderstruck II and Gypsy Rose.

The games run perfectly and look fantastic. Players will be able to quickly immerse themselves in the experience – great graphics, beautiful visual effects, and the accompanying sound effects all combine for instant enjoyment. 

Bizzo Casino Deposits & Withdrawals


One of this casino’s greatest strengths is the amount of deposit and withdrawal options available. It is possible to use both fiat and cryptocurrencies, with the minimum deposit being set to €10 (although a new player has to deposit double that amount if they want to get the welcome bonus).

Bizzo Casino deposit options allow players to choose between using their bank cards (Visa or MasterCard), one of the numerous wallets such as AstroPay, Skrill, ecoPayz, eZeeWallets, etc., or a cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Dogecoin, Tether, Ripple, Cardano). Even bank transfers are available!

The same goes for the withdrawal methods: the are are plenty to choose from, and the minimum requirement is once again €10, except for bank transfers (€35). However, there are daily, weekly, and monthly caps in this case (€4,000, €16,000, and €50,000, respectively). Bizzo Casino does not charge any fees, but this may not be the case with every payment method provider, so it may be wise to check that before initiating any transfers.

All requests are usually processed in a couple of hours, although the time it takes for the money to reach its final destination will depend on the payment method used. Still, the maximum waiting time is five days.

The casino also has account verification and KYC procedures in place to prevent money laundering. Completing these processes will allow players to fully utilize the speed with which the casino processes withdrawal requests. Otherwise, getting a request approved may take a while.

Bizzo Casino Interface & User Experience


Using this website is very easy and intuitive. The screen is never cluttered, but it’s never barren either – there’s always the right amount of items there to show the richness of the casino’s offer without it ever being overwhelming.

Finding the right game is also very simple because it is possible to search for a particular product using a search bar, but the games can also be filtered according to software providers or a particular type of game (slots, roulette, blackjack, etc.).

The customer service is also commendable, especially since one can reach the team via live chat. This makes getting any answer very quick and easy. If the live chat is not available, there is a contact form and the casino’s email address on the website. The reply usually comes the following day.

However, other Bizzo Casino reviews pointed out problems. Casanova.org noted that certain important information, like terms and conditions or bonus conditions, is hard to find, and Casino Guru found that certain sections of Bizzo’s terms and conditions seemed unfair to players.

We recommend familiarising yourself with every aspect of any casino site you visit, and Bizzo is no exception. We found their customer support team to be keen and knowledgeable, ensuring a safe and comfortable experience.

Does Bizzo Casino Have a Mobile App?


Bizzo Casino does not have a special app players can download and use to play the games that way but this is because there is absolutely no need for it.

The website works flawlessly on all mobile devices, so accessing it via a browser is smooth and effortless. In fact, the experience is just as good as on a desktop computer.

The only difference is that the main menu is at the bottom of the screen, not in a drop-down form in one of the upper corners.

Any player can access the website in a few taps and not lose absolutely anything when it comes to the gaming experience here.

Bizzo Casino Security


As mentioned at the start of our Bizzo casino review, this casino (owned by TechSolutions Group) has two different licenses: a Curacao Gaming Control Board license (number 8048/JAZ2017-067) and a Kahnawake Gaming Commission (number 00867). 

While the Curacao license is a standard in this industry, it is very commendable that the casino went ahead and acquired another license. This isn’t something seen very often, and it speaks volumes about the casino’s reliability that it was able to fulfill the criteria for multiple licenses.

Furthermore, this website has a valid SSL certificate, meaning all of the data on it is encrypted for maximum protection. All of the payment methods are reliable and work flawlessly and it’s possible to activate 2FA in a profile for an additional layer of security.

As this Bizzo Casino review has shown, there are plenty of very good reasons why the website has managed to maintain and even grow its presence since 2021. It is a reliable, fun casino where every player can find something appealing.

Most of the games are slots, but there’s also a great live casino to enjoy. Games like craps, bingo, and blackjack wrap up the selection nicely, and it’s worth pointing out that the providers available are some of the best in the industry.

But the most impressive features are the number of available payment methods and the fact that the casino has not one but two licenses.
