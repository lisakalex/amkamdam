What is Bovada Casino?

Bovada is an online gambling site that was launched in 2011. It’s based in Costa Rica, meaning it’s an offshore casino. This enables Bovada to accept players from the US, with the exception of Delaware, Maryland, Nevada, New Jersey, and New York. Bovada is also available in other countries.

Bovada offers a wide range of casino games. This includes table games like roulette, blackjack, and baccarat. There’s also an extensive selection of slots, video poker games, and live dealer tables. Bovada is also a popular sportsbook. It covers pre-match and in-play odds on dozens of sports, including football, tennis, basketball, and baseball.

Bovada also offers live poker rooms and a fully-fledged horse racing book. Our Bovada Casino review found that the platform is popular with bonus hunters. It offers several welcome packages across its main gambling products, plus occasional promotions for existing players.

In terms of payments, deposit methods include debit/credit cards from Visa and MasterCard. It’s also considered one of the best Bitcoin casinos, with various cryptocurrencies accepted. Although Bovada is based in Costa Rica, it’s authorized and regulated by the Governor of Curaçao. Its Curaçao e-gaming license is held by Bovada’s parent company, Harp Media B.V.

This section reveals each bonus and promotion currently available to Bovada players.

Bovada Casino Welcome Bonus Offers

Bovada offers welcome bonuses across its three main gambling categories; casino, sportsbook, and poker. Let’s break down each bonus type in more detail.

Casino Bonus

New players at Bovada will receive three casino deposit bonuses. However, the bonus amount depends on the deposit currency.

For example:

  • If you’re looking to deposit USD, you’ll get a 100% matched deposit bonus of up to $1,000. This can be claimed three times, valuing the bonus at $3,000.
  • If you deposit with cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin or Ethereum, you’ll get a larger welcome bonus. Each cryptocurrency deposit will be matched by 125% up to $1,250. This values the welcome bonus at $3,750.

Irrespective of whether you’re depositing USD or cryptocurrencies, you’ll need to enter a promo code. You’ll find these further down in this Bovada casino review.

Now for the important terms and conditions.

First and foremost, if you’re depositing USD, you’ll need to meet wagering requirements of 25x. This is based on the deposit and bonus amounts.

  • Suppose your first deposit is $500
  • Bovada matches it by 100%, so you’ll receive an extra $500
  • Your deposit and bonus amounts total $1,000
  • At 25x, this means you’ll need to wager at least $25,000 before you can withdraw the bonus funds

If you’re depositing cryptocurrencies, the wagering terms are slightly different. The first matched deposit comes with wagering requirements of 25x. Similar to the USD terms, this is based on the deposit and bonus amounts.

However, the second and third deposits need to be wagered at 50x.

  • Suppose your second cryptocurrency deposit is valued at $500
  • Bovada matches it by 125%, so you’ll receive an extra $625
  • Your deposit and bonus amounts total $1,125
  • At 50x, this means you’ll need to wager at least $56,250 before you can withdraw the bonus funds

So, while the cryptocurrency bonus is larger, the wagering requirements are less favorable. Another thing to note is that craps and live dealer games do not count toward the wagering target. What’s more, while slots contribute 100%, other games contribute less. Bovada doesn’t provide a bonus time limit within its terms and conditions.

Sportsbook Bonus

Bovada also offers a generous sportsbook bonus. Similar to the casino bonus, the amount you receive depends on the deposit currency.

  • If you’re depositing USD, you’ll get a 50% sportsbook bonus of up to $250.
  • If you’re depositing cryptocurrencies, you’ll get a 75% sportsbook bonus up to $750.

The key terms and conditions are similar across both bonuses. The wagering requirements are 5x when betting on sports or horse racing. However, this is based on the deposit and bonus amounts.

  • Suppose you deposit $200 into the Bovada sportsbook
  • You use USD, so Bovada matches it by 50%
  • This gets you an extra $100
  • Your sportsbook balance is now $300
  • At 5x, you’d need to wager $1,500 before you can make a withdrawal

The sportsbook wagering terms are a lot more favorable than the casino bonus. What’s more, the terms state that “You may use your bonus on any sport, wager type, and odds.” This means you can bet on sports markets with low odds, giving yourself a greater chance of meeting the wagering requirements.

Poker Bonus

Bovada also offers a welcome package for first-time poker players. Unlike the casino and sportsbook bonuses, only USD payments qualify for the bonus.

  • You’ll get a 100% matched deposit bonus of up to $500
  • You’ll need to download the Bovada poker software to activate the bonus
  • To convert the bonus funds to withdrawable cash, you’ll need to earn Reward Points
  • $5 is released for 150 Reward Points earned
  • You’ll earn Rewards Points every time you place a bet

There are no wagering requirements on released bonus funds.

Existing Customer Promos

Although Bovada offers generous welcome bonuses for new players, there’s a limited number of promotions for existing accounts.

This includes the Free Spin Bonanza bonus, which offers 500,000 weekly free spins. The maximum prize per player is just 20 free spins. However, there will be 25,000 winners every week. To enter, simply deposit at least $10. In the same week, you’ll also need to wager at least $20 on any casino game.

The only other bonus available to existing players is the Parlay Booster. Put simply, this gives you enhanced odds on selected parlay bets. You’ll also earn Reward Points, which get you access to the weekly Parlay Booster draw. However, the draw only allows you to earn additional Reward Points.

Bovada Casino Loyalty Program

Bovada offers a popular loyalty program that centers on Reward Points. You’ll earn Reward Points every time you place bets. However, the specific Reward Points earned per $1 depends on the gambling product. For example, you’ll get 40 Reward Points per $1 wagered on horse racing.

This increases to 60 Reward Points when betting on exotics and multiples. However, you’ll earn just 1 Reward Point when playing table games like roulette and blackjack. Slots are slightly better, with 3 Reward Points earned for every $1 wagered. You can also earn up to 15 Reward Points per $1 when playing live poker.

  • As you accumulate more Reward Points, you’ll move up the loyalty ladder.
  • There are six tiers, ranging from Starter to Hall of Fame. Some tiers have three levels.
  • As you increase your tier, you’ll get more USD for your Reward Points.
  • For example, the Starter tier requires 2,500 Reward Points for every $1 paid in cashback.
  • You’ll only need 1,000 to earn $1 when you reach the Hall of Fame tier.

Ultimately, if you’re a seasoned player who gambles regularly, it makes sense to join the Bovada VIP program.

Bovada Casino Deposits & Withdrawals

Understanding how payments work is crucial before signing up with Bovada. Not only in terms of deposit and withdrawal methods but limits and payout speeds.

Let’s start with the deposit process.

Bovada Casino Deposit Options

The most popular deposit method accepted by Bovada is debit/credit cards. Visa and MasterCard are supported but not American Express or Discover. The minimum and maximum deposit amounts when using debit/credit cards are $20 and $1,500, respectively.

You can also make a direct bank transfer in USD. The minimum is just $50. However, bank transfers are capped at $450. If you want higher limits, Bovada also accepts some of the best cryptocurrencies to buy. This includes Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, Ethereum, Litecoin, and Tether. The maximum deposit limit on these cryptocurrencies is $5,000.

In terms of deposit speeds, debit/credit cards are usually processed instantly. Cryptocurrency deposits take up to 15 minutes to appear. Bank wires can take several days.

Our Bovada Casino review found that deposit fees can also apply. Although the first deposit/credit card payment is free, you’ll be charged at least 15.9%. In contrast, Bovada doesn’t charge deposit fees when funding the account with cryptocurrencies. This further strengthens Bovada’s position as one of the best altcoin casino sites.

Bovada Casino Withdrawal Options

Not all deposit methods can be used to make withdrawals from Bovada. For example, while you can deposit funds with a debit/credit card, you’ll need to withdraw funds to another payment type.
