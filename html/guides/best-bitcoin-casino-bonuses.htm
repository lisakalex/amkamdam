Bitcoin Casino Bonus Terms & Conditions

Bitcoin casino bonuses come with a fair amount of fine print. It’s important to understand the restrictions on bonus offers and how bonus cash can be used. Here are some of the key terms and conditions to know:

Wager Requirement

The wager requirement is perhaps the most important condition every player should know about when using Bitcoin casino bonuses. This is the amount, relative to the amount of the bonus, that a player must bet in order to be able to withdraw their bonus funds.

As an example, say a $1,000 bonus has a wager requirement of 40X. That means that in order for the player to withdraw the bonus cash or any winnings earned by betting the bonus money, they must first wager at least $40,000 at the casino.

Game Restrictions

Some of the best crypto casino bonuses can be played on any games that a casino offers, but this isn’t usually the case. More frequently, bonus cash can only be used for certain types of games, like slots.

Many casinos restrict bonuses from being used on live dealer games. There may also be specific games or software providers for which bonus funds cannot be used.

Valid Period

Bonuses often don’t last forever. Casinos may require that players meet a bonus’s wagering requirement within a certain amount of time, like 30 or 60 days.
