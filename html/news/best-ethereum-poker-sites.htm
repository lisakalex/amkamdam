Ethereum Poker: Video and Live Compared


Video poker is a casino game played online with rules that are similar to those of live or online poker but with additional features. First and foremost, video poker is played against the dealer, and the odds are fixed.

Video poker has several advantages, one of which is how simple and intuitive it is to learn. To enjoy free Ethereum poker, players only need to adhere to a few straightforward instructions.

The first thing they need to do is choose the size of their bet. When ready, they simply press the deal button to begin the video poker session. If a winning hand is dealt, the player will win a multiplier, which is calculated against the stake.

For example, if the player gets a full house and this pays 100x, a $10 stake would yield winnings of $1,000.

Live poker, on the other hand, is played between players – just like in the real world. However, cards are dealt by software and players make hand decisions from the comfort of home.

Types of Ethereum Poker Games

Deuces Wild

One of the most popular video poker games is Deuces Wild. The game utilizes a single 52-card deck and has many variations, similar to Jacks or Better. By exchanging any handed card, they may be utilized to complete a winning hand.

Three-of-a-kind, which is the lowest hand in this type of video poker, usually pays out equally. A four-of-a-kind typically pays out at 5 times the wage, and a five-of-a-kind pays out at 15 times the bet.

Jacks or Better

One of the most popular and well-known variations of Ethereum poker is Jacks or Better. There are many pay tables for the game and all the best Ethereum betting sites offer it.

A ‘9/6 machine’ is the best table for the player, delivering a payback percentage every hand of 99.5%.

It pays 9 credits for a full house and 6 credits for a flush. The payouts for the full house and flush in other games are usually 8 and 5, respectively, decreasing player earnings and boosting the casino’s advantage.

Joker Poker

The majority of video poker games, including Joker Poker, have the same structure. However, there are jokers in this particular game, as the name of this wildly popular video poker variation suggests. A single joker, at least.

There are 53 cards in the deck including the joker. However, the joker serves as a wild card, making it a potentially advantageous card to possess. A royal flush, which consists of the 10, Jack, Queen, King, and Ace in a single suit, awards the highest payout in any video poker game.

Progressive Jackpot

Progressive jackpots accumulate over time until a player claims them. After that, they reset to a fixed level and begin to accumulate once more.

They do this by taking a portion of each wager placed on the game that the progressive jackpot is linked to and adding it to the total prize pool.

Many progressive jackpot games demand minimum bets in order to qualify.

Most poker games of this type also require the player to wager a certain amount in order to be eligible to claim the prize. As such, it’s important to check these details prior to playing.
