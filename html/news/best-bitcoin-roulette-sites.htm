Types of Bitcoin Roulette Games 

In this section, we briefly discuss the main types of Bitcoin roulette games that top crypto casinos offer. 

American Roulette

American roulette is the most popular variation in the US. There are two green zeros on the wheel which, when compared to European and French roulette, actually means that the casino has a higher house edge over the player. 

European Roulette

European roulette has just one green zero, which means players have access to a more favorable deal in comparison to its American counterpart. 

French Roulette

French roulette possesses the exact same wheel as the European version of this game. 

However, the key difference is that if the wheel lands on zero, players will receive 50% of their stake back on even money bets (e.g. red/black). 

This means that from an odds perspective, French roulette is the best version to play. 

Provably Fair Roulette

Provably fair roulette generates gaming outcomes via smart contracts. A hash seed will be taken from both the player and the casino and subsequently converted into a random roulette outcome. Each individual spin can be verified for its fairness via the blockchain ledger. 

Provably fair is, therefore, 100% guaranteed for its legitimacy. On the other hand, provably fair roulette games are often basic in nature, at least in terms of graphics and in-play bonus features. 

Live Bitcoin Roulette 

The best Bitcoin roulette sites offer access to live casino dealers. In turn, players can replicate a real-world casino experience without needing to leave their home. 

Here’s how live Bitcoin roulette games work:

  • The Bitcoin live roulette game will be provided by a leading gambling developer – such as Evolution
  • In this instance, Evolution will have an actual roulette wheel and croupier that will physically spin the wheel
  • There will be a high-definition camera that records the action in real-time which can subsequently be viewed by the player at the respective Bitcoin roulette site 

In terms of placing bets, this works in much the same way as a software roulette game. That is to say, the player will select their chip denomination on their screen and choose their preferred numbers. 

Once all bets are placed, the live croupier will then spin the roulette wheel.
