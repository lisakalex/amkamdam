What is Decentralized Gambling?

When playing at traditional online casinos, it’s necessary to complete the KYC process and enter bank or credit card details, for the purpose of deposits and withdrawals. 

Here are some of the main contrasts between traditional online casinos and decentralized gambling sites to clear the mist:

  • Platforms on the blockchain, such as the best decentralized casino sites we’ve talked about today, accept cryptocurrency deposits
  • Moreover, there is no need to complete the KYC process as per the regulatory obligations traditional casinos must follow
  • This means players can bet on sports fixtures and access casino games without entering personal banking information
  • Additionally, the decentralization of casinos eliminates the customary cost associated with conventional fiat banking practices
  • It also speeds up the deposit and withdrawal process for players

The majority of the decentralized crypto casinos we’ve reviewed for this guide are able to process deposits and payouts instantly.
