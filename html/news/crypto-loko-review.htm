What is Crypto Loko?


Crypto Loko is a crypto casino that accepts several different cryptocurrencies and offers a huge welcome bonus, which is what allowed it to attract a fair amount of players. Players can also participate in a loyalty program that has several tiers and offers some interesting prizes, plus take advantage of an indefinite 248% deposit bonus.

Poker is well represented, with about 15 variations, although other table games such as blackjack and roulette only have a handful of options.

Furthermore, the fact that Crypto Loko does not have a license should be mentioned – there is a robust security system in place to help protect the players’ privacy, but some the casino is not affiliated with any global regulator.

Crypto Loko Bonuses & Promos


Bonuses and promos are among this casino’s greatest strengths, so they deserve a careful in-depth view to see how they compare to the best crypto casino bonuses in the space.

Free spins, reload bonuses, competitions, and welcome bonuses are all available, but it’s good to keep in mind that some specific requirements have to be met first. Note that bonus amounts are always considered non-cashable.

To qualify for the Crypto Loko welcome bonus, players will have to deposit at least $20 after creating their account. They will also have to use the Crypto Loko bonus code GOLOKO before depositing to activate the bonus.

The bonus is huge, with 505% being added on top of the player’s deposited amount. In addition to that, 55 free spins on Neon Wheel 7s are also awarded. Another Crypto Loko bonus available to all players is the reload bonus. In this case, players have to use the bonus code ELGORDO and deposit at least $248. For that, they will receive a 248% slots bonus which has to be turned over 24x before it can be withdrawn.

Crypto Loko Loyalty Program

The Crypto Loko VIP program allows long-time players to pick up some great rewards, but to enter the program, one first has to make at least three deposits.

Loko Lounge has five different tiers, and the perks offered can be divided into three groups: insurance, Loko Points, and higher withdrawal caps. So, monthly insurance from losses can range from 5% to 10%, Loko Points can be awarded for every $10 spent to just every $2, and withdrawals can be executed in a blink of an eye, with the cap ranging from $2,000 to $10,000.
