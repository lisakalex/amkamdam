Bitcoin Casino Free Spins Terms & Conditions

At first glance, free spins strike as one of the biggest attractions offered by crypto casinos. But before players sign up for any free spin bonus, it is important to understand the terms and conditions associated with it.

In this section, let us take a closer look at what players need to bear in mind when reading the fine print of a free spin bonus. 

Wager Requirement  

Most Bitcoin casino free spin offers come with a wagering requirement. In simple terms, this is the number of times players must wager any winnings generated from the free spins offer, in order to be eligible for a withdrawal. 

For instance:

  • Suppose a player gets 10 free spins as part of a promotional offer. 
  • Let’s say that this comes with a 30x wagering requirement. 
  • After using the free spins, let’s assume that the player wins $100. 
  • However, before they can withdraw this $100, they should wager an additional $3,000 – which is 30x multiplied by the winning amount of $100.
  • Once the wagering conditions are met, then only players can withdraw any winnings left. 

Such high wagering terms can make the entire process somewhat challenging. It is not uncommon for casinos to stipulate wagering conditions as high as 40x or 45x. This is the case even for Bitcoin casino free spins no deposit bonuses. 

The wagering requirement will vary from one casino to the next, so make sure to read the terms of the bonus carefully before signing up.
