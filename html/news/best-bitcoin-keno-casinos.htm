Bitcoin Keno: How it Works

Bitcoin keno is the online version of the classic game, only players pay for their stake with BTC tokens instead of fiat funds. Players choose a specified amount of numbers and match as many as possible to those that are drawn – it’s comparable to a lottery.

The basic concept of keno is to choose numbers from a table and place bets on them in the hope of them being drawn. Like with lottery games, the more numbers a player correctly matches, the higher the winnings.

  • Most keno games have a grid containing up to 80 numbers and allow the player to pick up to 10. 
  • However, there are many other variations with different rules.
  • For instance, in Keno Rush, players choose up to 10 numbers from a grid for 40. 
  • In the Scribe version, players must choose up to 10 numbers from a grid of 36. 
  • Keno Goal, offered by BC.Game, allows players to select up to 20 numbers.

Some keno games are provably fair. For instance, at Lucky Block, all keno draws are carried out via smart contracts for fairness. As such, players can verify the results to ensure the keno draw was fair and true.
