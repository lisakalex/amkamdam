How to Pick the Best Crypto Horse Betting Site

Finding the best crypto horse betting site is not an easy task – especially when considering the wide range of options in the market. 

Nevertheless, players can narrow down their options by taking into account the following factors.

Security 

Players should make sure that their chosen crypto horse betting site has strong security measures in place. All of the casino sites from our list have adequate licensing to offer online gambling services. 

In addition to this, players can also check for other features, such as an SSL certificate and the latest security protocols that ensure data is kept safe.  

Horse Racing Betting Odds

It is important to compare odds from multiple online sportsbooks and bookmakers in order to find the most competitive prices. 

All of the best crypto horse racing betting sites we reviewed above stand out in the market with their often industry-leading odds. 

Moreover, players should also check whether their chosen racebook offers odds in their preferred format. 

For instance, some platforms might give odds in American or fractional format, while others might provide them in decimal. 

There are also online betting sites that support multiple odds formats, such as decimal, fractional, and money-line. 

Range of Horse Race Betting Markets

In simple terms, the more betting markets a site offers, the more wagering options a player has to choose from. 

As is clear from our guide, the best crypto horse race betting sites grant players access to a wide range of races from across the world. 

Moreover, players should check out what types of race betting markets are available on different sites. 

The top providers offer a wide range of horse race betting markets – such as win, place, show, exacta, quinella, trifecta, superfecta, and more. 

Racebook Bonuses 

Bonuses can significantly increase a player’s bankroll, allowing gamblers to play for longer periods or bet more than they would have otherwise. 

The bonuses can vary widely between different crypto horse race betting sites. 

Therefore, players need to think about what types of bonuses they prefer – such as matched deposits or free bets. 

It is also important to check the wagering conditions before joining a platform for the purpose of claiming a bonus. 

Live Streaming

Based on our research, there are not many betting sites that feature live streaming of horse races. 

Cloudbet is one of the few crypto horse race betting sites that feature this option. Moreover, there are also only a limited number of crypto casinos that facilitate in-play betting on races. 

Mobile App

Among the crypto horse racing betting sites we listed earlier, only 1xBit has a dedicated app. 

Otherwise, players can access their chosen horse betting site from their standard phone browser. 

Nevertheless, we noticed that all the top providers offer responsive websites that feature a smooth user experience, enabling players to bet on horse racing while on the go.  

Accepted Cryptos

Players should also consider whether the chosen horse race betting site accepts their preferred cryptocurrencies. 

While the majority of providers support Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Litecoin payments, funding with digital assets such as Cardano, Solana, and Dogecoin is not widely available. 

Min Deposit & Payout Time

When checking out the payment options, also assess the deposit requirements. 

For instance, on Lucky Block, players can get started with just $1, whereas the majority of other platforms stipulate $20 or more. 

Moreover, the minimum deposit conditions might also be different for specific bonuses. 

Payout times can also vary between betting sites – so it is best for players to choose a Bitcoin casino that supports instant withdrawals.
