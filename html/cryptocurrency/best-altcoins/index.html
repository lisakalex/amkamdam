What Is an Altcoin?


An altcoin is any cryptocurrency other than Bitcoin. These alternative coins were developed to offer features or capabilities that differ from Bitcoin’s original proposal.

Common examples include:

  • Ethereum, which supports smart contracts and decentralized applications.
  • Ripple, which is designed for high-speed, low-cost cross-border payments.
  • Cardano, which offers a secure and scalable blockchain environment.
  • Solana, which is known for fast processing times and low transaction costs.

During altcoin season, these tokens experience significant price increases, often outperforming Bitcoin. This usually happens when investors look for potentially higher returns by diversifying their crypto holdings beyond Bitcoin.

1. Ethereum (ETH) – Overall Best Altcoin to Buy

  • Market Cap: $463.33 billion

Ethereum is the top altcoin to buy in June 2024 due to its innovation, widespread use cases, and strong community. As the leading platform for smart contracts, Ethereum powers a vast array of decentralized applications (dApps) and decentralized finance (DeFi) projects, making it indispensable in the crypto ecosystem.

Recently approved Ethereum ETFs are driving increased investment and adoption. These ETFs provide new avenues for institutional investors, enhancing capital inflow into Ethereum. Experts predict a supply shock in the upcoming months. In addition, its transition to a Proof-of-Stake (PoS) consensus through Ethereum 2.0 enhances scalability and energy efficiency, solidifying its position as the best investment choice.

2. Binance Coin (BNB) – Best for Integrated Exchange Features

  • Market Cap: $103.82 billion

Binance Coin is one of the best altcoins to invest in due to its strong integration with the Binance exchange, the largest crypto trading platform globally. BNB offers significant benefits like reduced trading fees, access to exclusive token sales via Binance Launchpad, and cashback on Binance Visa card purchases. Its extensive ecosystem and consistent adoption make it a resilient investment choice.

BNB’s market resilience and liquidity are bolstered by its use on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), which supports a wide range of dApps and DeFi projects. BNB has surpassed Ethereum in terms of unique addresses, showcasing its growing user base and robust community support. The coin’s integration within Binance’s ecosystem ensures continued relevance and utility, making it a top contender for investors looking for stability and growth.

3. Chainlink (LINK) – Best Oracle Network

  • Market Cap: $10.21 billion

Chainlink is a top altcoin to invest in now due to its innovative oracle technology and broad adoption. As the leading decentralized oracle network, Chainlink securely connects smart contracts with real-world data, enhancing the functionality of blockchain applications across various sectors.

Chainlink’s Cross-Chain Interoperability Protocol (CCIP) and recent partnerships with major firms like BlackRock highlight its growth and utility. Its strong community, market resilience, and expanding use cases make it a robust investment. With its token trading at $16.42, Chainlink shows strong bullish momentum and significant future potential​.

4. Solana (SOL) – Best for High Speed Transactions

  • Market Cap: $79.52 billion

Solana is renowned for its high transaction speeds and low fees. The network can handle well over 2,000 transactions per second typically costing less than $0.001 per transaction, significantly outperforming Ethereum’s 30 transactions per second.

The Solana ecosystem is expanding rapidly, supporting diverse applications such as DeFi, non-fungible tokens (NFTs), and gaming. Solana’s robust infrastructure and strong community adoption make it a compelling investment. In the last year, Solana’s price has increased by +753.26%, highlighting its market resilience and potential for high returns.

5. Avalanche (AVAX) – Best for Customizable Blockchain Networks

AVAX’s strong community and robust infrastructure enhance its market resilience and liquidity. Recent price trends show significant gains, reflecting positive market sentiment. Its innovative platform and growing adoption across DeFi and NFT sectors make it a compelling investment.

6. XRP (XRP) – Best for Cross-Border Payments

  • Market Cap: $29.15 billion

Ripple’s On-Demand Liquidity (ODL) leverages XRP to facilitate instant and low-cost international money transfers. This positions XRP as a leader in the remittance market, attracting banks and financial institutions worldwide.

Moreover, Ripple’s launch of a USD-backed stablecoin on the XRP Ledger will enhance liquidity and introduce new use cases, boosting XRP’s utility. This development, combined with XRP’s strong market performance and significant adoption, makes it a compelling investment this year.

7. Dogecoin (DOGE) – Best Meme Coin

  • Market Cap: $23.61 billion

Dogecoin has a strong community and widespread adoption. As the original meme coin, Dogecoin boasts significant market liquidity and resilience. It continues to attract attention for its active community and use in tipping and charitable donations.

Despite the recent death of Kabosu, the Shiba Inu that inspired the Doge meme, Dogecoin remains a top choice for meme coin enthusiasts. Its consistent market performance and humorous origin set it apart from other cryptocurrencies.

8. Cardano (ADA) – Best for Robust Security

  • Market Cap: $16.37 billion

Cardano is known for its robust security and innovative technology. It uses a PoS consensus mechanism called Ouroboros, which ensures secure and energy-efficient operations. Cardano’s ongoing development, including the recent Voltaire upgrade, aims to make the network fully decentralized and self-sustaining, reducing regulatory risks and enhancing long-term stability.

Cardano’s strong community and adoption in dApps and NFTs add to its market resilience and liquidity. Its integration with various blockchains and focus on real-world use cases position it as a versatile and promising investment.

9. Tron (TRX) – Best for Decentralized Entertainment

  • Market Cap: $10.04 billion

Tron aims to decentralize the web, providing a platform for content creators to publish without intermediaries, thus ensuring higher profits and control. Its high throughput and scalability make it ideal for streaming, gaming, and social media applications.

Tron’s strong community and continuous innovation, such as partnerships with BitTorrent, enhance its market resilience and liquidity. The network’s efficient and low-cost transactions make it highly attractive for developers and users.

10. Notcoin (NOT) – Best for Growth Potential

  • Market Cap: $2.27 billion

Notcoin is a rapidly growing altcoin, known for its innovative approach to decentralized finance. It recently surged in price due to new partnerships and platform upgrades. Notcoin’s main use case is facilitating peer-to-peer lending, offering users a secure and efficient way to borrow and lend assets without intermediaries.

Its strong community and robust market liquidity contribute to its investment appeal. With a rising market cap and increasing adoption, Notcoin offers significant growth potential, making it an attractive option for investors seeking high returns.

The Advantages of Investing in Altcoins


Diversifying your crypto portfolio allocation with altcoins offers unique advantages. The main three are portfolio diversification, the potential for substantial price increases, and gaining exposure to different blockchain networks.

  • Enhances portfolio diversification: Investing in alrcoins spreads risk across different assets and reduces the impact of a single cryptocurrency’s volatility. Balanced gains offset losses from underperforming coins.
  • Offers a bigger potential for price increases: Altcoins have higher growth potential due to lower market cap. Conversely, Bitcoin’s high price limits dramatic growth opportunities.
  • Exposure to other blockchain networks: Investing in diverse technologies and use cases diversifies investment risks while unlocking opportunities in different sectors, which can increase potential returns.

The Risks of Investing in Altcoins


Investing in altcoins comes with risks, including a higher probability of failure, potential scams, and competition from emerging coins. Here’s how each of these risks can affect an altcoin’s long-term viability and market value:

  • Higher probability of failure: Altcoins’ experimental nature, smaller market presence, lower liquidity, susceptibility to market fluctuations, and greater operational challenges increase the odds of failure.
  • Increased risk of scams: Due to less scrutiny and oversight, altcoins are attractive targets for fraudulent projects. This increases the potential for deceptive practices and financial losses.
  • Competition from new coins: The constant influx of new crypto coins with superior technology or innovative features can render previous investments obsolete. Dilution of investor interest and capital across thousands of projects can also impact value and investment sustainability.

How to Choose the Best Altcoins to Buy Now


With there being so many different altcoins on the market, an investor needs to narrow down their search criteria to find worthwhile projects. Sometimes you need more than just the best crypto signals to know which crypto project is worth investing in. We’ll be sharing a few quick tips to help decide which altcoins to invest in.

Analyze the Whitepaper

Learning how to buy altcoins starts with assessing each whitepaper carefully. One of the most effective techniques to weed out lackluster projects is thoroughly reviewing a project’s whitepaper. The best altcoins to buy will always feature a well-written whitepaper that clearly explains exactly what the project does as well well as its plans for the future. If a cryptocurrency makes a lot of promises without explaining how they will be achieved, it’s best avoided.

See What Social Media is Saying

Taking a look at social media can be a great way to determine how investors feel about a project and whether it has a strong community. While stats like followers can be easily purchased online, it’s much harder to fake an active community consistently commenting and sharing so these are both factors to look out for when trying to find the best altcoins for 2024.

Look for Projects with Genuine Utility

While it’s possible for some projects to perform well and offer great returns through hype alone, it’ll never be conducive to long-term growth. Instead, it’s better to look out for utility-focused projects that provide a useful service or product. These altcoins will always have a demand regardless of hype or the market, making them far safer long-term crypto investments.
