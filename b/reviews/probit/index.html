Verification requirements and account tiers

Even though identity verification on ProBit is optional, having a verified account has additional perks like easier account recovery in case of losing a password or means of two-factor authentication (2FA) or higher 24-hour withdrawal limits. ProBit Korea users must verify their account if they want to make South Korean Won (KRW) deposits or withdrawals.

There are two key differences between verification on ProBit Global and ProBit Korea:

  • ProBit Global users can complete know your customer (KYC) check up to KYC Level 2.
  • ProBit Korea users can complete KYC up to KYC Level 4, which lets them invest in ProBit initial exchange offerings (IEO), as well as perform fiat (KRW) transactions.

In order to complete KYC, you simply need to go to your account section and click “Verification.” The exchange will guide you through the process and ask for the documents required to complete the process.

Basic Level 2 KYC check will ask you to provide:

  • Your nationality
  • Basic personal information such as name, gender, middle name, and last name
  • Date of birth
  • Address, including city, state, province, and postal code.
  • A photo of your ID or passport
  • A picture of yourself holding an ID or passport

ProBit ID verification is quick but can take several days at busy times.

ProBit fees

ProBit is considered to be a low-fee exchange, though trading fees are the primary source of revenue for the platform. Its fees and discount rates are subject to change according to crypto market conditions. Other revenue fees for the exchange include listing fees, IEO fees, withdrawal fees, and other fees associated with a planned implementation of new trading products.

ProBit’s effective trading rate ranges from 0.03% to 0.2%, and you can get an additional discount for staking ProBit's native PROB tokens. The exchange has 12 membership levels, which range from VIP 1 to VIP 11. Your membership level is determined by the amount of staked PROB tokens. The default trading rate at ProBit is 0.2%.

You can also pay trading fees using PROB tokens, which grants you an extra 0.02% discount per transaction. The effective trading rate of trade mining pairs ranges from 0% to 0.198%.

Compared with other exchanges, ProBit’s trading fees are low but not the lowest. For example, .com/reviews/binance/">Binance’s and .com/reviews/kucoin/">KuCoin’s lowest tier trading rate stands at 0.1%, while Poloniex will charge you 0.125% per trade. On the other hand, centralized exchanges like .com/reviews/bittrex/">Bittrex charge 0.2%, while you will pay up to 0.26% per trade when trading at .com/reviews/kraken/">Kraken. Meanwhile, .com/reviews/bitfinex/">Bitfinex charges its users 0.1% for market-making orders and 0.2% for taker trades. As such, ProBit is a median exchange in terms of trading fees.

As for withdrawal fees, they depend on the blockchain of the token being withdrawn. Each token listed on ProBit has a different withdrawal fee, which can be seen on its withdrawal page.

As you can see, ProBit’s fees are in line with the industry’s average. While they are higher compared with other crypto exchanges at a glance, ProBit offers a wide array of discounts and reward programs to ensure low fees for its loyal users.

A large number of those initiatives involve ProBit’s native PROB token.

ProBit’s PROB token

ProBit exchange has a native .com/coins/prob-probit-token/">ProBit Token (PROB), which is a standard Ethereum ERC-20 token. According to ProBit’s whitepaper, PROB is used for:

  • Trading fee discounts for Premium users (those who hold a designated amount of PROB).
  • Voting rights for listing new tokens based on the amount of PROB being held.
  • Increased referral bonus.
  • Early access to new features of the exchange.
  • .com/guides/what-is-an-initial-exchange-offering.htm">Initial exchange offerings (IEOs) running on ProBit’s IEO platform.

The maximum supply of PROB is 200,000,000. The tokens are said to have the following allocation.

According to ICO data site ICOBench, ProBit’s pre-ICO took place from 5th November 2018 to 28th November 2018. Meanwhile, its ICO took place from 11th December 2018 to 12th December 2018. ICO price per 1 PROB token was USD 0.20, and the team aimed to distribute 20,000,000 (10%) of the tokens for USD 4.5 million worth of cryptocurrencies. As per ICOBench, ProBit didn’t meet its hard cap and raised USD 110,000 worth of crypto.

100,000,000 (50%) of PROB tokens are allocated to trade mining and stake mining, meaning that exchange users can mine PROB tokens while trading or staking tokens on the exchange. For qualified transactions, 80% of the trading fee based on the current market price of PROB is mined and issued to the trader as a reward. However, trade mining does not apply to transactions in which the trading fee is covered with PROB tokens. Meanwhile, stake mining lets users stake PROB tokens and earn annual yield.

The main utility of PROB is fee discount, meaning that you can get either a 10% or 20 to 50% discount (based on your membership level) when paying fees with PROB tokens. The same applies to referral bonus, as you can get up to 10% or 20-50% bonus based on your membership level when your invitees make trades on ProBit exchange.

Besides, according to the whitepaper, ProBit is running a PROB repurchase program, where 20% of the exchange’s profits are used for repurchasing the tokens at the exchange. This practice will continue until 50% of total PROB (100,000,000) have gone through the repurchase program. The repurchased tokens are locked in the cold wallet and will be used as a crisis fund to cover ProBit’s expenses in unexpected situations.

It has a well-designed trading platform that is unlike other copycat exchanges that have proliferated the crypto market. Its slick platform comes with a variety of preset trading views that is likely to resonate with most of the crypto traders.

Looks aside, ProBit provides a top-tier performance trading platform that is capable of processing more than 1.5 million transactions per second, which makes using the platform a smooth and enjoyable experience.

Function-wise, the trading platform has all the typical features of a cryptocurrency exchange. It includes the following:

  2. Markets navigation panel

ProBit supports more than 340 cryptocurrencies at the moment and is known to be one of the first exchanges to list all sorts of new and hot tokens.

Aside from trading, ProBit makes it easy for its users to navigate between different sections of its exchange, including its IEO platform, trading competitions, exclusive offers, and events.

Trading competitions. Loyal ProBit traders can trade certain tokens and win prizes based on your performance. Mind that most of the competitions require you to have a certain amount of PROB stake to be eligible for entry.

All in all, ProBit provides quality service. Its platform is easy to navigate, and makes it simple to perform all sorts of operations, be it trading, staking, or participating in an IEO.

ProBit's security

As with most centralized crypto trading platforms, security at ProBit is of paramount importance. The exchange has no known security incidents thus far, which is reasonable, given its relatively short operating period.

The key pillars of ProBit’s security are:

  • Cold storage. ProBit keeps over 95% of user funds in its cold wallet, as offline storage is one of the best practices to prevent the loss or theft of digital assets.
  • Encrypted data. ProBit is said to use the strongest encryption algorithms for storing user data. As such, private information, as well as private keys are encrypted at least several times.
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA). ProBit allows its users to protect their accounts using software authentication methods like Google Authenticator, Authenticator Chrome Browser plug-in, andOTP, Enpass, Microsoft Authenticator, and Duo Mobile. Setting up a secure 2FA is considered a must to protect your account.
  • Universal 2nd Factor authentication (U2F). Users can also set up and lock their accounts with hardware keys by FIDO, making it impossible to get into the account without it. U2F devices are considered to be the most secure form of 2nd-factor authentication.
  • Entire session management. The exchange monitors all risky activity on the exchange and times out sessions when necessary.
  • Website security. ProBit usesHTTPS (SSL) everywhere on its site. Besides, it secures the exchange from malicious attacks like SQL injection, cross-site scripting, and DDoS attacks.
  • Physical software security. ProBit pays special attention to the safety of its facilities, including monitoring the server security and its management system.

As a result, ProBit is a highly secure exchange that is yet to experience a hack or a data leak. Its users can protect their account with robust security measures, meaning that you have an option to make it extra hard for scammers to drain your account. 

Nevertheless, ProBit remains a custodial exchange, meaning that you have to fully entrust it with the security of your funds. A recommended security practice is not to keep all of your funds on the exchange’s wallet and store it in a .com/guides/bitcoin-cold-storage.htm">private wallet instead.

In case you’re interested in maximizing your account’s security, read this security checklist written by ProBit’s security team.

Deposit and withdrawal methods

As of today, ProBit Global supports crypto deposits only, meaning that there is no way for you to buy or sell cryptocurrencies using fiat currencies. That said, you can make a deposit in all cryptocurrencies supported by ProBit.

ProBit Korea users can make KRW deposits and withdrawals directly to and from South Korean banks. However, to unlock this feature, all Koreans must verify their identity with the exchange.

Keep in mind that ProBit does not charge a fee for making crypto deposits, but there is a small network fee for withdrawing coins from the exchange’s wallet.

The withdrawal processing time depends on the cryptocurrency being withdrawn, as well as the busyness of the network.
