SEC Charges Impact Theory Over NFT Sales
The SEC has accused LA's Impact Theory of selling unregistered crypto assets in the form of NFTs termed “Founder’s Keys”. Between October and December 2021, they raised nearly $30 million by offering these to U.S. investors. The SEC’s Antonia Apps stressed the importance of securities registration. The case sees Impact Theory paying over $6.1 million in penalties.
